# 🌍 ESG Climate & Water Risk Analysis

A data-driven project analysing climate and water-related ESG risks across sectors using quantitative frameworks.

## 🔗 Live Site
[https://arnaverma18-droid.github.io/esg-climate-water-risk-analysis/](https://arnaverma18-droid.github.io/esg-climate-water-risk-analysis/)

## 📌 Overview
This project examines how climate-related physical and transition risks — as well as water scarcity and stress indicators — affect corporate ESG performance and financial outcomes across key industries.

## 🔍 Key Research Areas
- Physical climate risk exposure by geography and sector
- Water stress indices and corporate water dependency
- ESG rating sensitivity to climate-related disclosures (TCFD/ISSB)
- Materiality mapping for climate and water risks
- Scenario analysis (1.5°C, 2°C, 4°C pathways)

## 🛠 Tools & Frameworks
- Python (pandas, matplotlib, scikit-learn)
- Bloomberg Terminal / S&P Capital IQ for ESG data
- TCFD, ISSB S2, SASB frameworks
- WRI Aqueduct Water Risk Atlas
- IPCC climate scenario datasets
