# 🌍 ESG Climate & Water Risk Analysis

## Water & Carbon Risk Analytics for Sustainable Investment Portfolios

A Python-based ESG analytics project that estimates an investment portfolio's exposure to climate transition risk and water scarcity risk. The project demonstrates how environmental risk data can be integrated into portfolio analytics frameworks to support more sustainable and resilient investment decision-making.

While most ESG portfolio models focus primarily on carbon emissions, this analysis introduces a dual environmental risk framework that also incorporates water stress exposure, an increasingly important sustainability risk for sectors such as beverages, agriculture, utilities, and consumer goods.

## 📌 Overview
This project examines how climate-related physical and transition risks — as well as water scarcity and stress indicators — affect corporate ESG performance and financial outcomes across key industries.

## 🔍 Key Research Areas
- Physical climate risk exposure by geography and sector
- Water stress indices and corporate water dependency
- ESG rating sensitivity to climate-related disclosures (TCFD/ISSB)
- Materiality mapping for climate and water risks
- Scenario analysis (1.5°C, 2°C, 4°C pathways)

## 🛠 Tools & Frameworks
- Python (pandas, matplotlib, scikit-learn)
- Bloomberg Terminal / S&P Capital IQ for ESG data
- TCFD, ISSB S2, SASB frameworks
- WRI Aqueduct Water Risk Atlas
- IPCC climate scenario datasets
